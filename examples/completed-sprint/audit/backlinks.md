## Recommendations

1. **Build linkable assets.** The site has nothing worth linking to. Create: original research report, free tools (calculators, templates), comprehensive guides that serve as reference material.
2. **Digital PR outreach.** Target the 8 domains in the link gap analysis. Pitch guest posts to Mind the Product and ProductCoalition. Start responding to HARO queries in the analytics/product management categories.
3. **Fix broken backlinks immediately.** 3 links are being wasted. Blog migration (T1) will fix these.
4. **Do not buy links.** At DR 8, a sudden influx of purchased links is a red flag for SpamBrain. Build authority gradually through legitimate means.
5. **Target DR 20+ by end of sprint.** Requires ~15 new quality referring domains. Achievable with survey report + guest posts + HARO + linkable tool.
